Details of my Home Town

Oklahoma City is the capital of the U.S. state of Oklahoma. It's known for its cowboy culture and capitol complex, surrounded by working oil wells. The reflecting pool and empty glass and bronze chairs of the Oklahoma City National Memorial recall the victims of the 1995 bombing of the Alfred P. Murrah Federal Building. The Survivor Tree, an American elm nearly destroyed in the attack, is also part of the memorial.

Oklahoma City is home to several professional sports teams, including the NBA's Oklahoma City Thunder and the USL Championship soccer team, OKC Energy FC.Oklahoma City's economy is primarily driven by the energy sector, including oil and gas production, as well as by healthcare, technology, and aerospace. Some of the largest employers in the city include Tinker Air Force Base, the University of Oklahoma Health Sciences Center, and the Chesapeake Energy Corporation.
